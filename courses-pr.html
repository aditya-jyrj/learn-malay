Malay for Permanent Residency Applications

Language and Social Integration

Singapore values applicants who show social integration and language skills. Knowing English plus local languages like Malay can strengthen your PR application and boost your employability with multinational companies.

Our Malay for PR course offers an immersive introduction to Malay language and culture. In partnership with the Malay Heritage Centre, you'll enjoy guided visits and cultural activities.

We are beginner-friendly and open to all.
